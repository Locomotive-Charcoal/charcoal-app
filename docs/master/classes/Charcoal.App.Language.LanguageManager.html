\Charcoal\App\Language

\Charcoal\App\LanguageLanguageManager

Manage a collection of LanguageInterface objects and a unique TranslationConfig object.

Not implementing ConfigurableInterface because the AbstractManager shouldn't permit it's properties to be modified from outside the manager.
